Shipping Terms Guide

To sell globally on Muziris, buyers need to know exactly what is included in your price. Please use this simple guide to understand International Commercial Terms (Incoterms).

EXW
Ex Works (Factory Price)

Your Responsibility: You simply manufacture the goods and pack them safely in your factory/warehouse.

Buyer's Responsibility: The buyer sends a truck to your factory, picks up the goods, handles all export licenses, and pays for all shipping.

FOB
Free on Board (Port Price)

Your Responsibility: You manufacture the goods, transport them to your local seaport/airport, handle the customs export clearance, and pay to load them onto the buyer's ship.

Buyer's Responsibility: The buyer pays for the ocean/air freight to their country and handles importing.

CIF
Cost, Insurance, and Freight (Destination Port)

Your Responsibility: You handle everything in FOB, PLUS you pay for the ocean/air freight and shipping insurance to get the goods to the buyer's country port.

Buyer's Responsibility: Unloading the ship in their country, paying import taxes, and trucking it to their final warehouse.

DDP
Delivered Duty Paid (Door-to-Door)

Your Responsibility: You handle absolutely everything. You manufacture, export, pay for shipping, clear customs in the buyer's country, pay the buyer's import taxes, and deliver it directly to their door.

Buyer's Responsibility: They just wait for the delivery.

⭐ Recommendation for Small Manufacturers:

If you do not have an export license, or if you do not have a regular shipping agent (freight forwarder), we highly recommend quoting EXW (Ex Works). This allows Muziris or the buyer to handle the complicated logistics for you.
